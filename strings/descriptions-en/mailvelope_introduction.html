What is Mailvelope?

Mailvelope is for all users who want to read their mail directly in the web browser. Mailvelope is preconfigured for all common Mailproviders. So there shouldn't be problems with Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo! Mail, GMX, Posteo and some others.

Requirements:

Mailvelope is a free browser-plugin, for Mozilla Firefox and Google Chrome. You can download them by clicking at the links down below. Mozilla Firefox,
