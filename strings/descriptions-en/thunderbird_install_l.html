Most GNU/Linux operating systems come with GnuPG installed on them, so you do not have to download it. Before configuring GnuPG though, you'll need the IceDove desktop email program installed on your computer. Most GNU/Linux distributions have IceDove installed already, though it may be under the alternate name "Thunderbird." Email programs are another way to access the same email accounts you can access in a browser (like Gmail), but provide extra features.

Setup your email program with your email account

Open your email program and follow the wizard (step-by-step walkthrough) that sets it up with your email account.
