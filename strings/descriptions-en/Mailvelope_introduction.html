What is Mailvelope?

Mailvelope is for all users who want to read their mail directly in the webbrowser. Mailvelope is pre configured for all common Mailproviders. So there shoundn´t be problems with Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo! Mail, GMX, Posteo and some others.

Requirements:

Mailvelope is a free browser-plugin, for Mozilla Firefox and Google Chrome. You can download them by clicking at the links down bellow. Mozilla Firefox: https://www.mozilla.org/de/firefox/new/
Google Chrome: https://www.google.de/chrome/browser/desktop/
